Welcome to Festive Flavors

Festive Flavors is a visually appealing storytelling of recipes from India. Some are traditional, forgotten, and others are modern as we learn daily. We created these step by step guides so that a new cook can also try these dishes, and the experienced cook can also share their ideas, or for anyone to just have fun watching them. We started this journey for the love of food, recipes which we learned from our grandmothers and they from their grandmothers adding techniques with each generation. Here's to a tasty future, from the hearth of our ancestors, to your kitchen. Enjoy!
